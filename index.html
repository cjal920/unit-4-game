CRYSTAL COLLECTOR!

The object of this game is to make your score match the target number by collecting crystals. And here's how you do that. The precious stones at the bottom of the screen each have a numerical value. Your job is to select the right stones in the right quantities so that your score adds up to the target. How do you know each stone's value? Well, you don't. Like market values, like the target you're aiming for, they fluctuate. And each game they're different. Only by clicking them will you know their value. But do be careful. Just as the only way to win this game is by matching the target with your score, the only way to lose is by exceeding it. Like The Price Is Right. So don't aim too high! And good luck!
