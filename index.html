BIDDER BEWARE!

The object of this game is to make your bid match the target price. And here's how you do that. The precious stones at the bottom of the screen each have a current market value. Your job is to select the right stones in the right quantities so that your bid equals the target price. How do you know each stone's value? You don't. Like all market values, like the target price, they change with each game. Only by clicking them will you discover their value. But do be careful. Just as the only way to win this game is by matching the target price with your bid, the only way to lose is by exceeding it. Just like The Price Is Right. Good Luck!
